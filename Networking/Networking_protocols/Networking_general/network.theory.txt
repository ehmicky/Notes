VPN:
  - accessing a private network from public
  - private network can be a proxy, to provide anonymity

Network:
  - According to size: 
     - PAN (Personal Area Network), for a room
     - LAN (Local), for a building
     - CAN (Campus)
     - MAN (Metropolitan) for a city
     - WAN (Wide), for wider, e.g. the internet
  - According to privilege:
     - Internet: everyone
     - Intranet: only within an organization
     - Extranet: by an organization for business partners
